We Care

Home Care Agency in Windsor Ontario

Comfort Care provides a personalized home care experience for Canadians, empowering them to select their care worker and determine the care schedule. We aim to provide a best-in-class experience for Occupational Therapists, Nurses, Adjusters, Case Managers, and Legal Assistants looking for care for their clients.

Comfort Care provide wide range of home care services and manages a range of health conditions so the client can stay at home as long as possible while meeting their needs. Comfort Care impacting how people live and age at home, today and into the future. The company is dedicated to enhancing the quality of life, dignity and independence of all Canadians.



Mission

With Comfort Care, it’s all about living life independently with the right care and support to meet your needs.
